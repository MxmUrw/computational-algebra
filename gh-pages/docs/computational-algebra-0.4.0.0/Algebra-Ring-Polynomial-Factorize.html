Given that f = gh (mod m) with sg + th = 1 (mod m) and leadingCoeff f isn't zero divisor mod m, henselStep m f g h s t calculates the unique (g', h', s', t') s.t. f = g' h' (mod m), g' = g (mod m), h' = h (mod m), s' = s (mod m), t' = t (mod m), h' monic.
